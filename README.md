# Sophora Capital LLC

## About Us

Sophora Capital LLC is a U.S. registered limited liability company providing international commercial advisory and strategic coordination services.

We focus on supporting cross-border projects in the areas of:

- Business structuring advisory
- Commercial documentation support
- International market research
- Technology and IP coordination
- Strategic partnership facilitation

## Our Approach

We work with clients in the United Kingdom and Asia, providing advisory services for early-stage and growth-stage ventures seeking international expansion.

Sophora Capital LLC does not provide regulated investment, fund management, or financial brokerage services.
